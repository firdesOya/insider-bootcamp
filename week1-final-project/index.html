The Big Bang Theory

8.1/10 • 2007 - 2019 • Sitcom

The Big Bang Theory is a sitcom that follows two genius physicists, Sheldon Cooper and Leonard Hofstadter, as they navigate their socially awkward yet hilarious interactions. Their geeky world takes a turn when their new neighbor, Penny, and their friends bring unexpected changes into their lives.

Favorite Scene

Sheldon's Unforgettable Moment Watching His Own Video

When Sheldon watches a video he recorded in the past, he admires himself like a true scientist. Following his own principle, "If it doesn't come out, go in!", this scene remains one of the most hilarious and genius moments in the series.
